Topic sentence

Pregunta Verdadero-Falso

Listen to what a topic sentence is and answer the questions

Pregunta 1

The main idea is stated in  the supporting sentences.

Retroalimentación

Falso

The main idea is stated in the topic sentence

Pregunta 2

The topic sentence tells the topic of a paragraph.

Retroalimentación

Verdadero

The topic sentence tell the topic to be discussed in the paragraph.

Pregunta 3

The topic sentence is always the first sentence in a paragraph.

Retroalimentación

Falso

It is not always the first sentence.
